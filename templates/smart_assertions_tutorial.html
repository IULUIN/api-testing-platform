📖 断言类型参考

类型 说明 示例
equals 值等于预期值 {"type": "equals", "path": "code", "expected": 200}
contains 值包含指定字符串 {"type": "contains", "path": "message", "expected": "success"}
greater_than 值大于预期值 {"type": "greater_than", "path": "age", "expected": 18}
less_than 值小于预期值 {"type": "less_than", "path": "price", "expected": 1000}
regex 值匹配正则表达式 {"type": "regex", "path": "email", "expected": "^.*@.*\\.com$"}
exists 字段存在 {"type": "exists", "path": "data.id"}
type 检查数据类型 {"type": "type", "path": "name", "expected": "string"}
length 检查长度 {"type": "length", "path": "items", "expected": 10}
